Productivity Challenge : Delta

This is a newer addition to the challenge albeit an optional one. Recommended for those who have completed the challenge before and want to push themselves to greater heights.

The Aim of Vision is to bring out your truest potential.It is rightly said that it takes 21 days to form a habit and following that principle, the principal aim is to bring life altering changes in this 21 days challenge. Being in a group where you are held responsible for your actions every single day will push you harder and make you work. This will help in getting better and stronger than your excuses each day. Above all, it is the perfect opportunity to mould yourself into your best self.

This is an upgrade to the Productivity Challenge : Basic

How it can benefit you:

    • It can help you to improve your concentration.
    • It can help you to focus on your goals without interruption.
    • You can grow stronger, better and mindful.
    • Can optimize your mind-set, improve alertness, bring motivation.
    • Having a clear plan for your day helps you make the most of your time and actually accomplish the important things in life.

These rules must be followed to complete the challenge successfully:

  • Wake up before 8 am

Getting up early means more hours in the day to work, relax, and plan.

  • Read 10 pages of any book or article

Self-help books, autobiographies, and articles give you a broader aspect of life. They might help you through adverse times when you learn via those who have faced similar challenges!

  • Physical AND mental exercise

Training the physical body and soul at the same time will do wonders for an individual. You will move several steps closer to a happier lifestyle.

  • Planning the day

This is the most important rule and will stay the same in challenge delta. But, planning needs to be done strictly before 10 am to make sure you have time to complete them. 60% of the tasks are to be completed. Whether you have completed the tasks in your planner will also be checked.

NOTE: There will only be a single strike for those undertaking the Challenge Delta!
