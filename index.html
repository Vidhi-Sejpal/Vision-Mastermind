About Us

"It's amazing how even just a week or month of diligent focus, commitment and discipline can make such a difference. Never underestimate what you can achieve when you go all in."

The Aim of Vision Master Mind is to bring out your truest potential. It is rightly said that it takes 21 days to form a habit and following that principle, the principal aim is to bring life altering changes in this 21 days challenge. Being in a group where you are held responsible for your actions every single day will push you harder and make you work. This will help in getting better and stronger than your excuses each day. Above all, it is the perfect opportunity to mould yourself into your best self.

It is a community where people come together with a common aim of succeeding in life. Every member of this group strives to achieve the goals they set for themselves and also ensures that the other members are in check. It is a challenging environment where sticking to oneâ€™s word is all that matters.

You may originate your own plans in accordance with certain rules that are to be followed. People in this group should be willing to assume full responsibility for their actions for the period of the challenge. A strong will to achieve the set goals and a burning desire to finish the challenge is highly promoted here.This is a place to thrive and bring out the best in you, which may have been lost or not found yet at all.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can join?

    Anyone who is ready to follow the 4 rules and achieve their set goals in a period of 21 days with uncompromising dedication.

  • What will I get out of this?

    The pleasure and sense of fulfillment after smashing all your goals and having a definite daily routine. Self improvement is the greatest achievement! Along with this, if you finish the challenge, we will reward you with a certificate. Winners might get some exciting goodies too!

  • Do I have to pay anything?

    No, this is totally free and open to anyone who is serious about the same.

  • What if I don't get accepted?

    Your applications will be very carefully studied, as this challenge is to be taken seriously. If, for some reason, you cannot continue, you can drop out. You can always apply again in the coming batches, they run monthly.

  • How can I collaborate with Vision?

    We would love to collaborate with you, please get in touch with us regarding the same.
