Rules and Guidelines

Foundation Rules of the challenge to be followed every day:

  • Wake up before 9am

    • An early start to the day improves your concentration and helps one to focus on goals and tasks without interruption. Getting up early ensures one has set a morning routine.

  • Read 10 pages of any book or article

    • When one reads an inspirational book every day, their brain floods with positive messages and uplifting concepts. Even if you don't particularly like to read, this is how you grow stronger, better and mindful. Even if it's a struggle initially, it's worth one's time to read an inspirational book every morning or at whatever time works best for you.

  • Physical or mental exercise

    • Any form of exercise that suits you best comes under this rule. The goal is to optimize your mind-set, improve alertness, increase attention and bring motivation. Girls experiencing chums between the challenge should meditate and not miss this step.

  • Planning the day

    • This is the most important rule, as your achievements cannot be bigger than the plans you have. Planning is the ticket to achievements. It’s hard to feel organized and on top of things if you’re unprepared. Having a clear plan for your day helps you make the most of your time and actually accomplish the important things in life.

A journal, planner, or any notebook is a must here.

Some Guidelines to note:

  • It is crucial to follow the above mentioned rules and if you fail to do so for more than 2 days in the course of the challenge, you will no longer be able to continue as a part of the Master Mind group.
  • A drive link will be provided which will be accessible to all the group members. Each member of the group will get a separate folder in which daily activities will be logged. Details of the same shall be explained later.
  • No spam or promotional content of any sorts is allowed.
  • If a person falls sick during the challenge, feel free to notify the same and drop out of the challenge. Health is of top priority.
  • Members in the group are encouraged to talk freely about their goals, failures, achievements, setbacks, or anything else that is relevant to this theme.
  • Members of the group are free to check any other member’s drive folder and call them out if they feel that they are slacking off or not following the rules
  • Workout or mediation of minimum 10 mins each day is to be done. Any time of the day is fine.
  • Plans for each day should be uploaded to the drive before 11am. If there are any changes, the same can be updated before 6pm.
  • You are wholly responsible to keep yourself in check as this program is made for your own self improvement.
