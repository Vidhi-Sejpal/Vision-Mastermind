Mo-Bye Challenge

The world of technology is so great that it allows us to reach the other side of the earth at the press of a button on a device that can fit in our pockets comfortably. Things that humans couldn't even think of doing 100 years ago are so easily done now.

Alas, the very tool that was made intending to help us do tasks in a short period of time is the one that takes most of the time of our lives. On an average, a teenager spends 9hrs of their day looking at their phone. That accounts for over 1/3rd your entire day!

The sole purpose of the challenge is to limit your phone usage to a maximum of 7hrs.

Why Mo-Bye?

We know how difficult it can be for you to give up such a lucrative and addictive piece of technology but we also believe in the power of productivity. Wasting time on the phone and being productive do not go hand in hand. We want you to become a better you, 'A Productive You'. We will serve as a leash of accountability and be the eyes of a Warden to keep you in check and guide you through this challenge.

Why charge?

Money is the most dynamic invention by man. Not only it is used for buying and selling commodities, but is also a powerful motivator. We would like to call the cost of this challenge an investment. We won't double your money and neither you will face risks as in crypto.

We can provide you with something which no other investment can, that is TIME. This investment returns time, as well as at no loss. You will get your money back if you successfully complete the challenge.

The challenge has just one simple rule:
  • Less than 7hrs screen time.

Spending a maximum of 7hrs on screen time means that you have 2hrs more than an average teenager to spend on valuable productive things. This will serve as a tremendous advantage for you over others.

Though you can decide your threshold of maximum screen time, we have kept a cap on it as 7hrs.

Why 7Hrs?

As an average teen spends 9hrs using a phone, imagine what you can do with at least 2 extra hours! You can play an instrument, catch up with your family, exercise, meditate, draw, read, start a novel, learn a language, and chase your dreams. The possibilities are endless! You can utilize this time in doing things you always wanted to do but ended up wasting your time watching some video or sharing memes.

NOTE: There will only be a single strike for those undertaking the Challenge Mo-Bye!
