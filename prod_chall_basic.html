Productivity Challenge : Basic

Habits are the essential foundation for any productivity practice. Everyone has goals but with the right push and a sense of responsibility, the drive to work harder intensifies multifold. With that in mind, Vision welcomes a group of driven people who wish to take the initiative and are ready to be committed fully and completely to this challenge.

The Aim of Vision is to bring out your truest potential.It is rightly said that it takes 21 days to form a habit and following that principle, the principal aim is to bring life altering changes in this 21 days challenge. Being in a group where you are held responsible for your actions every single day will push you harder and make you work. This will help in getting better and ber than your excuses each day. Above all, it is the perfect opportunity to mould yourself into your best self.

These rules must be followed to complete the challenge successfully:

  • Wake up before 9am and put a 'good morning' text on the daily updates channel of your respective groups.
  • Plan you day and put up a screenshot of the same before 11am . ( If you want you can make any changes in the planner before 6 pm )
  • Workout for minimum 10 mins and put a screenshot in your personal folder. ( Showing your face is not mandatory ,any random picture of your yoga mat, youtube video or shoes etc will work)
  • Read minimum 10 pages of any book (fiction, non-fiction whatever you like).
  • As part of this challenge you have to decide on one habit that you want to gain or lose . once you have decided on the habit you have to start working on it everyday and upload a screenshot as proof . For eg if you decide to eat one bowl of fruits everyday then you will put a snap of your fruit bowl etc
